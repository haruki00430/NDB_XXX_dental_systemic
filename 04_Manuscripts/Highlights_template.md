# Highlights（提出用テンプレート）

誌面が求める箇条書き（通常 3〜5 行・字数制限に合わせて短縮）向けのたたき台です。

- Prefecture-level NDB open data (FY2023) link periodontal disease claim rates to diabetic complication management (B001-20) across Japan (N = 47).
- Associations are robust in multiple OLS specifications; a caries negative control supports specificity versus generic dental burden.
- HbA1c-based outcomes show null associations, consistent with health-checkup selection and exposure measurement issues discussed in the text.
- E-value and spatial analyses contextualize unmeasured confounding and residual spatial structure in residuals for the primary outcome.
